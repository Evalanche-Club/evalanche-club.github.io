The EvalanChE Club

"For the students, by the students"

Welcome to EvalanChE Club

The EvalanChE Club is a vibrant community within the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Jain (Deemed-to-be) University. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration among students through technical and cultural events.

Explore Our Events Learn More About Us Get in Touch

Vision

To create a platform where students can explore, learn, and showcase their talents, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications. We envision a community that nurtures innovation, creativity, and leadership, empowering students to become well-rounded professionals and responsible citizens.

Mission

To provide a comprehensive ecosystem for students to:

  1. Enhance technical skills through workshops, seminars, competitions, etc.
  2. Develop soft skills through events like debates, hackathons, paper presentations, trainings, etc.
  3. Foster cultural awareness and social responsibility through cultural days, sports days, social services, etc.
  4. Encourage networking, team building, and professional communication.
  5. Provide a platform for personal growth, stress management, and overall well-being.
